2030 Vision

Unending Growth Towards Sustainability

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

POVERTY

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

HUNGER

The eradication of hunger, securing food supplies, enhancing nutrition, and advancing sustainable agriculture.

HEALTH

The ensurance of healthy lives and promote
wellbeing for all at all ages

EDUCATION

The ensurance of inclusive and equitable quality education and promotion of lifelong learning opportunities for all

GENDER

Achieve gender equality and empower
all women and girls

WATER

Ensure availability and sustainable management
of water and sanitation for all

ENERGY

Ensure access to affordable, reliable,
sustainable and modern energy for all

ECONOMY

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable
economic growth, full and productive
employment and decent work for all

INDUSTRY

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation

INEQUALITY

The reduction of social inequalities
within and among all countries

COMMUNITY

Make cities and human settlements inclusive,
safe, resilient and sustainable

CONSUMPTION

Ensure sustainable consumption and
production patterns

CLIMATE

Take urgent action to combat climate
change and its impacts

AQUATIC LIFE

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, and marine resources for sustainable development

LAND LIFE

Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss

JUSTICE

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all and build effective, accountable and inclusive institutions at all levels

COOPERATION

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development
