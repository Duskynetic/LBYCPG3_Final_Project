18-Day Campaign to End Violence Against Women

By honoring its commitment to combat all kinds of gender-based violence as contained in the 1987 Constitution, the Philippine government is able to further its aim of defending the human rights of women and girls. This goal is supported by the 18-Day Campaign to End VAW.

Governmental organizations are required to raise awareness about the issue of violence and the elimination of all forms of violence against women and girls in accordance with Republic Act 10398, also known as the Act designating November 25 of each year as the National Consciousness Day for the Elimination of violence against women and children (VAWC).
