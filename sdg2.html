National Feeding Program

Outgoing President Rodrigo Duterte signed the Republic Act No. 11037 or the Masustansyang Pagkain Para sa Batang Pilipino Act last June 2018. The government is required by law to offer a variety of services, including supplemental feeding programs for children in daycare, school-based feeding programs for public school students in kindergarten through sixth grade, a milk-feeding program, a micronutrient feeding program, health checks, immunizations, and deworming, among others.

The RA 11037 caters to undernourished kids attending public kindergartens, childcare centers, and elementary schools.

Early Childhood Care & Development F1K

Under the ECCD-F1K, the Development First 1000 Days was launched last 2016. The first 1000 days of life or the first two years of a child's life are covered by the ECCD-F1K. This time frame has been referred to as the "golden window of opportunity" since it is at this time that health, nutrition, and early learning interventions will have the greatest impact on children's physical and mental development. The package covers services for pregnant women and young children under the age of two in the areas of nutrition, health, early learning, and social services. Ten of the poorest and most important provinces in the nation will adopt it.
